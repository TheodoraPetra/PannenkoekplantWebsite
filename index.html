The story began with one pannenkoekplant I bought on an early spring day in Amsterdam. I must have been lucky because that plant has been growing and growing every since, producing little plants that I've been giving away across the Netherlands and beyond.

The first few plants went to friends, then slowly my pannenkoek family started overgrowing my 1 bedroom apartment, and there I was, selling a few plants to local plant lovers and asking them to send me pictures of their pannenkoek growth in their new house.


One day I asked myself: How nice would it be to be able to have a "map" of the entire plant family? So putting my beginner coding skills to test (with valuable help from a special someone) I embarked on a journey to track the legacy of that very first pannenkoekplant

You are one of the people that has received one of my plants, either directly from me or via someone that owns a member of the "family".

I invite you to register your pannenkoek below, and explore where the rest of the family currently resides. You might be surprised of how far they made it :)
